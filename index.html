Inventory optimization · Data centralization

Bistro, retail & stock in one member-aware view

Unified dashboard (demo) · POS + roster + inventory

Tie F&B and retail lines to the same member key as dues and check-ins—so attach rate, lane-level spend, and reorder alerts stay in one place instead of three silos (membership, POS, spreadsheet). Production builds use a small warehouse or curated tables (Snowflake, BigQuery, or Postgres) fed by exports/APIs on a schedule you control.

What “centralized” means here

Demo model: each sale line carries member_id (when not a walk-in guest), joined to program and tenure from your roster; on-hand quantities roll up to SKUs shared with the front desk. One dashboard filters the same dimensions—membership tier, month, product category—across people and product.
