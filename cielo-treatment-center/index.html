How to read this preview

Same patterns as our live portfolio (Chart.js, filters, tabs). Nothing here touches your EHR. It is a conversation starter for a pilot after we map exports or read-only connections you approve.

First pilot scope often looks like:

  • Census and capacity by track (evening IOP, co-occurring, DUII, etc.) against group slots.
  • Referral and intake funnel in aggregates (calls, assessments booked, admits).
  • Staffing vs. scheduled demand so overtime and bottlenecks show up before they harden.
  • Optional later: AR aging and denials beside census when billing wants the same picture.
EHR stays in place Reporting sits on top of exports or approved connectors (SimplePractice, Valant, Kipu, billing, etc.). No rip-and-replace.
Ops-first metrics We focus on access, utilization, and wait times unless you define clinically validated outcome measures you want tracked.
